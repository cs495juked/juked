What is Juked?

Juked is an application to give the power of music to the people. When a group of friends are together, the task of picking the music they listen to typically falls to one person. The host of a party or gathering can’t relax and enjoy their company because of the stress of constantly choosing music everyone will enjoy.

Juked allows the host to share this responsibility by allowing the entire group to pick their favorite songs and vote on their friends’ choices so everyone gets to listen to music they like. Juked only allows you to have one song on the playlist at a time, so that one friend with horrible taste in music isn't playing Nickelback all night. Juked also allows you to upvote your favorite songs so you hear them ASAP, and downvote your least favorites to remove them from the playlist. Aside from making a party more interactive, the app also provides opportunity for people to venture out of their comfort zone and discover new music.

  • Play what you like
  • Discover new music
  • Collaborate
  • Be a part of the party

How Juked Works

1

Create

If you are a party host, you will create a new lobby and be given a code unique to your party. If you're a guest, simply ask the host for the lobby code and enter it in the app to join.

2

Login

The party host will use the Juked app to log in to their Spotify and/or Soundcloud account and allow guests to choose from their libraries. Don't worry - no matter what songs the party picks, Juked won't mess with your Discover Weekly (or any other finely tuned Spotify based playlists).

3

Jam

Guests and the host can now all add music to the playlist, vote on their favorites, and get the party started.
